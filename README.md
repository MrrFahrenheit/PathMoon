# PathMoon 🌙🕶️

**PathMoon** is an interactive learning platform designed to deliver a seamless and intuitive experience for mastering new topics. By leveraging cutting-edge AI integration and dynamic learning paths, PathMoon transforms the way curiosity becomes expertise.

---

## 🚀 Core Objectives

* **Simplicity & Intuition:** Delivering roadmaps that are easy to navigate and understand, regardless of the topic's complexity.
* **Efficiency First:** Every generated path is optimized to align perfectly with your specific learning goals, ensuring no time is wasted.
* **AI-Powered Guidance:** Utilizing the **Vercel AI SDK** to create a living curriculum that adapts to your needs in real-time.

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

### 1. Dynamic Roadmap Generation
Based on any user request, PathMoon generates a structured roadmap divided into core topics. Each topic features:
* **In-depth Informative Content:** AI-curated theoretical foundations.
* **Curated Media:** 3 hand-picked YouTube resources per topic.
* **Mastery Gates:** A customized AI-generated assessment that must be passed to unlock the next level.
* **24/7 AI Tutor:** Ask the AI specific questions about the topic at any stage of the level.

### 2. Tailored Learning Depth
Choose the journey that fits your schedule and ambitions:
* **Fast-Track (Basic):** Focus on the essentials.
* **Standard (Intermediate):** Master the core skills.
* **Comprehensive (Complete):** Total mastery through a full curriculum.

---

## 📈 Engagement & Tracking

### 🧠 Retention Tools
* **Daily AI Reviews:** Adaptive daily exams designed to reinforce previous topics, moving knowledge from short-term to long-term memory.
* **Weekly Performance Insights:** A comprehensive feedback loop summarizing check-in consistency, topics studied, and milestones achieved.

### 🎨 Visual Experience
* **Animated Tracking System:** An engaging UI with real-time feedback to keep you motivated.
* **Visual Mastery Tracking:** Intuitive progress bars and color-coded status signs (Neon Cyan, Deep Purple, and Lunar Gold) for a friction-free experience.

---

## 👥 Social & Profile
* **Profile Management:** Track your personal growth and historical achievements.
* **Social Sharing:** Share your progress, custom courses, and media with the community to learn together.
